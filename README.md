# Favorite Movie
## The Fast and the Furious

**Details**
1. The Fast and the Furious franchise is 16 years old.
2. The Fast and the Furious was known as Redline, which refers to the maximum speed to which a car can accelerate. That name wasn’t terribly catchy, and producers came up with The Fast and the Furious.
3. Director Rob Cohen visited real illegal street races in preparation for the first film in order to bring authenticity to the screen.
